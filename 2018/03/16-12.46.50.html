Paradoxical feeling

Imagine a transporter that has a 99% chance of doing nothing and a 1% chance of working correctly. It can make transport attempts 1,000 times per second.

You have 3,000,000 of these working together.

How many transports complete after 1 second?

3,000,000 * (1-0.99) = 30,000
