iOS or Android?

"What's best" always depends on what you want to do. This is my impression of which of iOS and Android is better in different circumstances:

Lowest costAndroid
Any specific single feature (best camera, dual-sim, FM radio, etc.)Android
Best build qualityiOS
Easy to modifyAndroid
Secure from other people modifying it without you knowingiOS
Smart WatchiOS
Smart Watch if you don't like the Apple WatchAndroid
Virtual RealityAndroid
Voice control interfaceAndroid
MacOS integrationiOS
Meaningful choice of web browsersAndroid
